Codify

The Coding Contest

Event Overview

  • Event Name: Codify
  • Date: July 12th, 2024
  • Duration: 3 hours (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM)
  • Participants: Expected number of participants is 60
  • Platform: Coding challenges will be hosted on platforms like HackerRank and LeetCode
  • Registration Fee: 50 INR per participant
  • Venue: Computers will be provided at the contest location

Contest Format

  • Individual Event: Codify is an individual competition. Each participant will compete separately.
  • Problem Statements: The contest will include problems of mixed difficulty levels.
  • Scoring: Participants will be scored based on the points they earn on the coding platform.
  • Tie-breaking: In case of a tie, the winner will be determined based on the time taken to complete the challenges.

Registration

  • Registration Fee: 50 INR per participant.
  • Deadline: Participants must register before the event date.

Contest Schedule

  • Start Time: 10:00 AM
  • End Time: 1:00 PM

Participation

  • Each participant must register individually.
  • Participants must be present at the venue at least 30 minutes before the contest starts.

Scoring and Ranking

  • Scores will be based on the points earned on the coding platform.
  • In case of a tie, the participant who completed the challenges in the least amount of time will be ranked higher.

Prize Distribution

  • Prizes will be awarded based on the final ranking.
  • Winners will be announced at the end of the contest, and prizes will be distributed accordingly.
