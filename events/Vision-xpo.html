Vision Xpo

Event Overview

  • Event Name: Project expo
  • Date: July 12th, 2024
  • Duration: 4 hours (1:30 PM - 5:30 PM)
  • Participants: Limited slots of 30 projects
  • Registration Fee: None
  • Venue: Dayananda Sagar Academy of Technology and Management

Exhibition Details

Participants can exhibit their innovative projects to the judges and attendees. Each participant is expected to bring their project on the day of the event and provide explanations to the judges. The exhibition will showcase a variety of projects across different domains.

Contest Schedule

  • Date: July 12th, 2024
  • Start Time: 1:30 PM
  • End Time: 5:30 PM

Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated based on innovation, creativity, functionality, and presentation. Judges will select the top three projects based on their assessments.

Participant Limit

  • Only 30 slots are available for project exhibition.
  • Participants must secure their slots on a first-come, first-served basis.

Prize Distribution

Winners will be announced at the end of the exhibition. Prizes will be awarded based on the judges' decisions.
