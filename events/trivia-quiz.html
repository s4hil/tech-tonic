Trivia Quiz

Event Overview

  • Event Name: Trivia Quiz
  • Format: Team-based competition with teams of 3 members each
  • Date: July 12th, 2024
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Participants: Expected number of teams is 30
  • Registration Fee: 150 INR per team
  • Venue: Dayananda Sagar Academy of Technology and Management

Contest Structure

  • Rounds: The quiz will be conducted in multiple rounds, with eliminations occurring after each round until three winning teams are selected.
  • Topics: Questions will cover topics related to the IT industry, latest technology, companies, and related fields.

Registration

  • Team Size: Each team must consist of 3 members.
  • Registration Fee: 150 INR per team.
  • Deadline: Teams must register before the event date.

Contest Schedule

  • Date: July 12th, 2024
  • Start Time: 2:00 PM
  • End Time: 5:00 PM

Team Composition

  • Each team must consist of exactly 3 members.
  • Substitutions or changes in team composition are not allowed after registration.

Scoring and Ranking

  • Teams will be scored based on the number of correct answers.
  • In the case of ties, tie-breaking questions will be used to determine the rankings.
