About

Get ready for an exciting challenging 3 day extravaganza! TECHTONIC is not just a tech fest; it's an immersive experience designed to ignite the spark of curiosity, foster innovation, and celebrate the dynamic world of technology. This grand event is a melting pot of creativity, intellect, and groundbreaking ideas, where students converge to explore, learn, and showcase their prowess in diverse technological domains.

To participate register online, Limited slots available.

Themes

Code Arena

Code Arena is a hackathon event that brings together enthusiastic programmers, developers, and tech enthusiasts to engage in collaborative challenges and problem-solving.

Idea X

Idea X is a collaborative event that brings together participants with diverse backgrounds and skill sets to pitch innovative ideas and solutions within a specified time frame.

Codify Fix

Codify Fix is an engaging and competitive coding event where participants showcase their programming prowess in a high-pressure environment.

Trivia Quizzix

Trivia Quizzix is an engaging and entertaining event that combines the elements of trivia and quizzing. Participants, either individually or in teams, gather to test their knowledge across a wide range of topics.

Vision Xpo

Vision Xpo is an innovative event that serves as a platform for participants to showcase their cutting-edge projects. This expo focuses on highlighting technological advancements and creative solutions.

PerSpecta

Perspecta is an engaging and dynamic event that brings together tech enthusiasts and experts to participate in lively debates about various technology topics.

Gunfire Glyphs

An electrifying gaming event that brings together avid gamers from all walks of life for an intense competition to determine the last team standing across a variety of games.

Artify

Artify is a vibrant and innovative event that brings together participants from diverse backgrounds to engage in the creation of digital art.

Project Expo

An adrenaline-fueled fusion of cutting-edge tech and high-speed excitement at the RoboRace! Immerse yourself in the future of motorsports, AI-powered race cars take the track, navigating twists, turns, and challenges with lightning-fast precision.

Robo Race

Robo Race is an engaging and competitive event where participants showcase their skills in designing, building, and demonstrating robotic projects.

StockDash

Dive into the dynamic world of stocks with StockDash, the exhilarating 3-day online trading event at TechTonic. Join us for an immersive experience where financial markets meet technology.
