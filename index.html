About

Get ready for an exciting and challenging 24-hour hackathon event! Code Arena brings together college students from all over to compete in four domains: App/web development, IOT-based projects, Gaming related challenges and Cyber Security. Participate for a chance to have fun, gain knowledge, and win amazing prizes!

To participate register online, Limited slots available.

Themes

DevFest

Embark on a coding adventure where you can craft captivating web applications, showcase your talent, and connect with like-minded individuals. This competition isn't just about coding; it's about turning your innovative ideas into reality.

IoT Nexus

Dive into the thrilling realm of the Internet of Things (IoT) competition, where creativity meets connectivity. Imagine transforming everyday objects into smart solutions that revolutionize the way we live. This is your chance to pioneer the future, creating innovative IoT solutions that transcend boundaries.

Gamejam Galaxy

Dive into the thrilling world of competition, where creativity meets technology in an electrifying dance of pixels and code. Unleash your imagination, craft captivating virtual worlds, and challenge yourself to bring your gaming vision to life.

Cyber Security

Cybersecurity competitions are the modern battlegrounds for those passionate about protecting the integrity of the digital landscape. It's a place where quick thinking, strategic prowess, and technical mastery collide to thwart cyber threats. Join this high-stakes adventure, where you'll unravel intricate scenarios, defend against simulated attacks, and emerge not just as a competitor but as a guardian of the virtual frontier.

Rules & Regulations

  • Reporting Time: All participants must arrive at the venue one hour before their allocated time on the event day(8:30am).
  • Event Duration: Code Arena is a 24-hour event starting from 11 AM on Day 1 to 11 AM on Day 2.
  • Meals: Lunch and Dinner will be provided on Day 1, and breakfast will be provided on Day 2. Snacks will also be provided.
  • Essential: Participants must carry their college ID cards, Laptops and all the required hardware parts must be brought by the team only, organizers will not be liable to provide it.
  • Theme: The Hackathon has various domains, allowing participants to work on any project idea they choose. Note : The participants registered for Web and App Development will be working on the given problem statements
  • Accommodation: All participants must stay in campus, and accommodation facilities will be provided for the night.
  • Certificate: E-certificates will be issued to all participants as acknowledgment of their participation.
  • Registration Fee: The registration fee is non-refundable and depends on the event.
  • Team Size: Teams can have 2-3 members. No members can be added/removed from the team. Team should be the same as during the sumbission done before.
  • Code of Conduct: Participants must maintain professionalism and ethical behavior. Cheating, plagiarism, or any unethical practices will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Completion: Projects must be completed within the given time frame.
  • Collaboration: Teams must work independently, collaboration with other teams is not allowed.
  • Judging Criteria: The judging criteria, including innovation, technicality, usability, and presentations, will be communicated to participants before the judging process.
  • Organizers Decision: The decisions made by the organizers and judges will be final and binding.
  • Code Usage: Teams can use libraries, frameworks, or open source code, but using pre-existing projects solely for the purpose of the event is not allowed.
  • Malpractice: Participants are encouraged to report any malpractice to the judges for fair evaluation.
